Frequently Asked Questions

What is an ECR (Elite Chest Run)?

An Elite Chest Run is a big group that moves through elite zones to open elite chests and “tag” elite enemies. Elite zones are areas with tougher mobs and higher level loot. Tag means you hit the mob once so you get credit for the kill and the drop. The run follows a set route so everyone stays together and clears fast.

What is “Infix” gear? And what is an Infix Elite Chest Run?

Infix gear is the current open world drop pool. These items roll from a specific set of perks and stats that players call the Infix pool. An Infix Elite Chest Run is a route built to farm elite chests and elites that drop this Infix gear. You join the run, open every elite chest on the route, tag the elites, and collect Infix drops.

How do I join an ECR on my server?

Watch recruitment chat for “ECR,” “WT” for world tour, or “+myrk.” Type “+” to join when a leader posts a group. Meet at the listed shrine or town. Bind the nearby shrine, then follow the marker or the leader. Stay with the zerg. A zerg is a very large group moving as one. I think on NA it's * instead of + in chat.

Can I do elite chest runs solo?

You can solo some parts, but it is slower and riskier. Many elite chests sit inside camps with dense mobs. A small group of 5 to 10 makes it easier. A full zerg is fastest. If you must solo, pick safer loops, use a mobile build, bring healing and defense consumables, and skip bosses that waste time.

What drops from Infix ECRs?

You get Infix weapons and armor, including named items. You also get crafting materials, refining reagents, and consumables. Recipes and patterns can drop. You gain weapon mastery and salvage a lot of gear for coin and parts. The mix changes by zone and your luck, but the goal is steady Infix gear and useful mats.
